Welcome to William Shakes Pear

This website is a UX experiment that blends Shakespeare’s universe with a single, very confused pear. Explore plays, poems, and sonnets; search for lines and words; and see how far one fruit can go in the world of classic literature.

What you can explore

Catalogue
Browse all plays, poems, and sonnets with summaries and filters for type, genre, and chronology.
Find the Line
Paste a line of Shakespeare and discover which work it’s from, who said it, and where it appears.
Word Frequency
Type a word to see how often it appears across Shakespeare’s works, and which characters love it most.
Pear Lab
A playful corner for random pear facts, generated scripts, and other experiments that may or may not make sense.
